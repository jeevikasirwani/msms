About Us

I am Ganesh Kad working as President of MSM Basketball Sports Association and Aurangabad District and Taluka Basketball Association, quite happy and unhappy also. I personally struggle not from childhood but from higher education years, already it was late. Though I struggle and achieved and recognized as basketball player on state and national level. But mine ambitions are much higher and what I need is a coach and so many talented basketball players to play on national and international levels. At this stage, I worked and achieved record as player, coach and official of basketball on state, national and international level. Still I personally think that ai achieved it but I am not satisfied in my works. As an organising secretary I successfully completed so many and lot of district, state, national levels basketball tournaments. Now I want to organise so many national and international level basketball tournaments and so many related Campaign in the region. With also to work as Head Coach of . Maharashtra State and as well as Indian team coach un future. Try and try again and than it will success. Ganesh Kad, President, MSM Basketball and ADTBAA.
